April Direction

Strategy Adjustments

Three moves for April — grounded in March data.

1

Doubling Down

Keep scaling the 30:30 format content

Retention & Comments · Community Goal

Retention is at 72% — the highest signal we track. More 30:30 explainer and first-timer experience Reels will sustain this and push into broader discovery.
2

Testing

Test childcare-focused content to drive shares

Shares & New Followers · Awareness Goal

Shares (22) are below target. Content that speaks directly to the "I want to work out but I have kids" objection activates the share reflex among Cincinnati moms — the core growth audience.
3

Fixing

Add save CTAs to class-prep and workout-tip content

Saves · Community Goal

Saves (6) are well below target. Practical content — what to wear, what to expect, how to prep — with an explicit "save this" CTA is the fastest path to moving this metric.
