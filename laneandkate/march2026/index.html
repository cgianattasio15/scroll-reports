April Direction

Strategy Adjustments

Three moves for April — grounded in March data.

1

Doubling Down

Scale aesthetic macro and lifestyle jewelry content

Views & CTR · Discovery Goal

The top post was a close-up macro carousel — visually arresting and highly shareable. This format consistently outperforms for fine jewelry and should anchor the April content mix.
2

Testing

Test "how to choose your ring" educational content

Saves & New Followers · Trust Goal

Saves dropped sharply MoM. Decision-stage educational content — cut comparisons, metal guides, custom process explainers — drives saves from high-intent buyers.
3

Fixing

Add explicit share CTAs to proposal and engagement content

Shares · Awareness Goal

Shares are below target. Proposal-adjacent content with a "tag your partner" or "send this to someone who needs a hint" CTA is the most direct path to more shares in this niche.
