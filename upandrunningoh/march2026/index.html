Strategy Adjustments

Three high-priority moves for April — grounded in March’s data and mapped to your core business goals.

1
Doubling Down

Scale the expert fitting and running education content

Why

Profile Visits (249) are on track — the content is driving profile exploration. Educational content on shoe fitting, injury prevention, and training plans positions Up & Running as the expert running resource in Columbus and drives saves.

↗

What This Moves

More education content → saves above Spark floor → warm audience that returns to the profile and converts to in-store fit bookings.

Testing

Why

Shares (11) are below the Spark floor. Local running community content (race recaps, trail spotlights, local athlete features) activates the share reflex among the Columbus running community and drives discovery among cold audiences.

↗

What This Moves

Shares above Spark floor → expanded local reach → new followers from the Columbus running community.

3
Fixing

Add direct booking CTAs for in-store fit appointments

Link Taps & CTR · Conversion Goal

Why

Link Taps (6) are below the Spark floor. The in-store fit appointment is the primary conversion event for Up & Running — content that names the specific service, the benefit ('find your perfect shoe in 20 minutes'), and the booking link will reduce friction and drive more conversions.

↗

What This Moves

Link Taps above Spark floor → more in-store fit bookings → higher customer lifetime value.
