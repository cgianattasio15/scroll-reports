April Direction

Strategy Adjustments

Three moves for April — grounded in March data.

1

Doubling Down

Scale team and community spotlight content

Views & New Followers · Awareness Goal

The team spotlight post drove 12,491 views — the highest of any content this month by a wide margin. More human-first stories (staff, customers, local runners) will sustain this reach.
2

Testing

Test "training tip" educational reels

Saves & Retention · Trust Goal

Saves are below target. Practical running tips — form, gear, race prep — with a "save this for your next run" CTA drives saves from the core audience and builds authority.
3

Fixing

Add share CTAs to event and community content

Shares · Community Goal

Shares dropped sharply MoM. Event announcements and community content with a "share this with your running group" CTA is the most direct path to more shares in this niche.
