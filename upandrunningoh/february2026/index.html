March Direction

Strategy Adjustments

Three high-priority moves for March — grounded in February’s data and mapped to your core business goals.

1
Doubling Down

Make founder and staff story content a monthly non-negotiable

Reach & Comments · Awareness Goal

Why

The Feb 5 “Nineteen years ago” founder story drove 15,157 views and 104 comments — by far the highest-performing post in the account’s history. The community responded to the personal, origin story format with exceptional depth of engagement. This is the account’s breakout format and should be a consistent monthly pillar, not a one-off.

↗

What This Moves

Reach and comments sustained at the February peak → new follower acquisition from warm, community-first audiences → accelerated path to Lift Stage readiness.

2
Doubling Down

Lead every new brand launch with a dedicated Carousel post

Shares & Engagement · Awareness Goal

Why

The Feb 13 Salomon launch Carousel drove 66 shares and 15.6% engagement — the highest shares count of the month and a clear signal that new brand arrivals are the shares engine. Running enthusiasts tag their partners and share gear content at a high rate. Every new brand arrival deserves its own dedicated launch post with an explicit “Share with your running partner” CTA.

↗

What This Moves

Shares consistently above the On Track floor → expanded organic reach to new running audiences → more in-store traffic from gear-curious, purchase-ready customers.

3
Fixing

Add explicit Follow CTAs to high-reach content to convert views into followers

New Followers · Community Goal

Why

Avg Reach/Day exceeded the Spark ceiling at 1,772/day — but New Followers only grew by 27. The account is getting massive distribution without converting viewers into followers. The gap between reach and follower growth is a CTA problem, not a content problem. High-reach posts need an explicit “Follow for more [brand] drops and local running events” CTA to close the loop.

↗

What This Moves

New Followers above the Watch floor → growing local running community → stronger foundation for the Lift Stage transition in Q2.
