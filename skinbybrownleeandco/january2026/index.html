What We Learned

Key signals, wins, and gaps from January that inform February’s strategy.

Win

Authority Content Is the Reach & Engagement Engine

The Jan 12 Authority Builder Reel drove the highest views, saves, shares, and retention of the month. The formula is confirmed: PSA-style hook + specific actionable education + urgency framing. This is the template to replicate 2–3 times per month in February.

Win

Views Crossed the Rise Floor for the First Time

Total Views hit 181,257 in January — the first month above the 150K Rise floor. Combined with the reach improvement (+65% MoM), this signals the account is beginning to build the distribution foundation needed for the Rise stage.

Signal

7 WATCH Badges Reflect Rise-Stage Targets, Not Poor Performance

This account moved to Rise-stage targets in October 2025. Rise targets are significantly more aggressive than Lift — they’re designed to reflect where the account needs to be, not where it is today. A 8.0/10 with 7 WATCH badges at Month 4 of Rise is a healthy trajectory. The account is building toward those targets, not failing to hit them.

Watch

Saves Need Consistent Save-Optimized Formats

Saves improved 2.6x MoM (43 → 112) but remain below the 300 Rise floor. The Jan 12 Authority Builder drove 32 saves — the format is confirmed. The fix is frequency: at least 2 save-optimized posts per week with explicit “Save this routine” CTAs.
