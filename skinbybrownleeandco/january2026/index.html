Key Insights

What the Numbers Tell Us

Four strategic patterns that matter most for February planning

Win
Conversion Infrastructure Proves Audience-Offer Alignment

Your CTR (16.35%) and PCR (17.79%) both significantly exceed Rise Stage targets. This validates that when qualified prospects reach your profile, they engage and convert at exceptional rates. The conversion path is proven—the infrastructure works. The growth opportunity is traffic volume, not conversion optimization.

Growth Lever
Awareness Gap: Reach Below Rise Floor

Average reach of 2,078/day falls below the 5,000 Rise Stage minimum. With 32K followers, this indicates limited content distribution. The algorithm isn't amplifying your content at scale. February priority: test high-shareability formats (transformation stories, authority-building tips, myth-busting POVs) to trigger algorithmic favorability and expand organic reach.

Opportunity
Engagement Depth Signals Format Optimization Need

Saves (112), shares (77), and retention (39%) all fall short of Rise targets. Content is converting visitors but not inspiring saves or shares at scale. The 1/12 post (32 saves, 35 shares) proves the audience WILL engage deeply with the right format. Test tighter hooks, listicle-style delivery, and transformation-focused storytelling.

Momentum
Quality Metrics Show Upward Trajectory

Despite volume challenges, engagement quality improved significantly: watch time +47%, saves +70%, comments +177% MoM. When people see your content, they're increasingly engaged. This validates content direction—now the priority is distribution. Scale what's working through format testing and pillar amplification.

Format Performance

What's Working (and What Needs Work)

Three format verdicts that inform February content direction

✓ Working
Authority Builder (Expert Tips)
32 saves · 35 shares · 3,638 views (Jan 12)

Why it works: The expert tips format leverages Sylvia's 17+ years of credibility while delivering immediately actionable advice. The "you're throwing money away" hook creates urgency through loss aversion. This format earns saves (reference content) and shares (helping friends) simultaneously while positioning Sylvia as the trusted perimenopause skincare expert.

February Direction: 2× per month minimum. Lead with pattern-interrupt hooks that create urgency or challenge assumptions. Keep lists to 3-5 items for retention. Follow each post with Instagram Stories amplifying one tip in detail.
✓ Working
Transformation Story / Case Study
31 comments · 18 saves · 3,377 views (Jan 9)

Why it works: Before/after content is visual proof that validates expertise. The high comment count (31) shows this format sparks conversation—people ask questions, tag partners. Transformation content answers the key buyer question: "Will this actually work for me?" Social proof at scale.

February Direction: 1× per week. Always include the transformation timeframe and specific treatments used. End with a clear CTA (Skin Quiz or consultation link). Use Story Highlights to organize transformations by concern (acne, hyperpigmentation, aging).
⚠ Needs Adjustment
Educational Carousel
Low saves across multiple posts despite solid reach

Why it needs work: Carousels are reaching audiences (1,900+ views) but generating minimal saves. Content is being consumed but not bookmarked. The framing treats these as "educational content" when they need to be positioned as "pre-purchase tools" or "reference resources." Lack of urgency or utility in the hook.

February Direction: Reframe hooks for save-worthiness: "Save this before your next treatment" or "Bookmark this: everything you need to know about..." Target: 10+ saves per carousel. Test adding a downloadable/screenshot-friendly final slide.
February Strategy

Four Strategic Priorities

The focus areas that will drive the greatest impact next month

1
Test High-Shareability Content Formats
Goal: Increase shares from 77 to 150+ and reach from 2,078/day to 3,500+/day

Tactics: Myth-busting POVs ("What derms won't tell you about..."), before/after transformation carousels with shocking results, founder vulnerability moments. Focus on formats that prompt "send this to your friend dealing with hormonal skin" behavior. Double down on authority-builder format that drove 35 shares in January.

2
Optimize First 3 Seconds for Retention
Goal: Improve retention from 39% to 50%+

Tactics: Pattern interrupt hooks (start mid-sentence, use on-screen text that creates curiosity), immediate problem-solution framing. Test 5 different hook styles in first 2 weeks: question-based, pattern interrupt, bold claim, "before I tell you X", list-based ("5 things..."). Analyze which drives highest retention.

3
Amplify Conversion Path with Quiz-Focused Content
Goal: Maintain 16%+ CTR while increasing profile visits to 2,500+

Tactics: Educational carousels ending with Skin Quiz CTA, product comparison content that positions the quiz as the decision tool, ingredient deep-dives. Leverage proven conversion rates (17.79% PCR) by driving more qualified traffic to profile through value-first content.

4
Establish Founder POV as Authority Pillar
Goal: Build differentiated authority through consistent Sylvia-led content

Tactics: Weekly founder POV addressing perimenopause skin misconceptions, behind-business transparency (what it's really like running an esthetics practice), expert counter-points to influencer skincare trends. Position Sylvia as the trusted voice vs. generic wellness creators—17+ years of licensed expertise matters.
