April Direction

Strategy Adjustments

Three moves for April — grounded in March data.

1

Doubling Down

Scale founder POV and lesson-learned Reels

Views & New Followers · Awareness Goal

The top 3 posts this month were all founder-voice formats — vulnerability, humor, and contrarian authority. These are the formats driving the reach breakout. More of these in April will sustain the 170K+ views trajectory and push follower growth past 500/month.
2

Testing

Add explicit “save this” CTAs to educational content

Saves · Community Goal

Saves (152) are below the Rise floor of 300. Educational content — ingredient breakdowns, routine guides, myth-busting carousels — with an on-screen “save this for your routine” CTA is the fastest path to moving this metric.
3

Fixing

Test shorter Reels (under 20s) to improve retention

Retention % · Engagement Goal

Retention (24%) is significantly below the Rise floor of 65%. The reach is there — viewers are finding the content — but dropping off early. Tighter hooks, faster payoffs, and shorter formats will improve completion rates and signal quality to the algorithm.
