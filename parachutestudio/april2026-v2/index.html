Production Model

The script-led remote model

Because Parachute is in Dallas and we're in Cincinnati, we run a script-led production model in place of our standard on-site shoots. This isn't a workaround — it's a better fit for a founder-led creative studio.

Creative directors and brand strategists understand visual direction. When your team executes our scripts with your own camera, the content carries an authenticity that produced agency content can't replicate. It's the difference between a brand that talks about creativity and one that visibly lives it.

Founder-led studios with strong visual instincts execute scripts exceptionally well — and audiences respond to it. The content feels like it came from inside the studio because it did.

Revision structure

Month 1 (strategy) allows up to 3 rounds as we calibrate voice. Month 2+ is capped at 2 rounds per batch, with feedback required within 48 hours. This keeps the system efficient and maintains publishing momentum.
