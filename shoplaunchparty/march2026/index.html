Doubling Down

Scale content that drives comments and profile visits

Testing

Saves · Trust Goal

Saves (45) are below target. Beauty education content — ingredient breakdowns, routine guides, product comparisons — with an explicit 'save this' CTA is the fastest path to moving this metric.

Fixing

Create shareable content tied to identity and values
