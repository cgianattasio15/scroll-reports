Analysis

What We Learned

Key signals, wins, and gaps from February that inform March’s strategy.

Win

CTR Continues to Strengthen — Conversion Path Is Compounding

CTR improved for the second consecutive month (12.50% → 14.12% → 15.14%). At 2.5× the Lift ceiling, this is the clearest signal that the content is attracting the right audience and the profile is converting. The conversion infrastructure is working — the growth lever now is reach volume.

Win

Opinion-Led Content Is the Shares Engine

The Feb 26 “Shopping small” Reel drove 118 shares — more than double the next-highest post. Opinion-led hooks that challenge assumptions or take a clear stance are the primary organic distribution mechanism for this account. This format needs to be systematized, not treated as a one-off.

Watch

Reach Needs Consistent Hook-Driven Distribution

The Feb 3 Reel hit 5,349 views — but the remaining 9 Reels averaged under 1,700 views. The distribution gap is a hook problem, not a content quality problem. Every Reel needs a curiosity-gap or identity-challenge hook in the first 2 seconds to replicate the Feb 3 breakout performance consistently.

Signal

Watch Time Improving — Content Depth Is Working

Watch Time improved to 7.10s (+0.54s MoM) despite lower reach. This means the audience that does watch is staying longer — a sign that content depth and storytelling are landing. The goal now is to get more people into the top of the funnel so more of them experience that depth.

Strategy Adjustments

Three strategic adjustments taking effect in March, grounded in February’s performance data.

Rationale

  • The Feb 26 “Shopping small” Reel drove 118 shares — the highest of the month and a clear signal of the format’s reach potential
  • Opinion-led hooks outperform informational hooks for shares and cold reach on boutique retail accounts
  • Shares are the primary organic distribution mechanism for this account at Lift stage

Expected Outcome

  • Shares sustained above the 60 floor in March
  • Avg Reach/Day improvement toward the 1,000+ range

Doubling DownHook Strategy · Reach Fix

Rationale
